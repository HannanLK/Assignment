Are you looking for a comfortable and affordable stay in Nuwara Eliya or Sigiriya? Whether you are traveling solo, with a partner, or with your family, we have the perfect room for you. Choose from our three types of rooms, each with its own features and benefits.


Single Standard


A cozy and budget-friendly room for one person with a single bed, a desk, a wardrobe, and a private bathroom. Enjoy free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and a flat-screen TV with cable channels. Ideal for business travelers or solo adventurers.



Double Deluxe


A spacious and luxurious room for two people with either one double bed or two twin beds, a sofa, a coffee table, and a private bathroom with a bathtub. Enjoy free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, a mini-fridge, a microwave, and a flat-screen TV with cable channels and Netflix. Ideal for couples or friends.



Triple Executive


A large and elegant room for three people with either one double bed and one twin bed, or three twin beds, a work desk, a lounge chair, and a private bathroom with a shower. Enjoy free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, a mini-bar, a coffee maker, and a flat-screen TV with cable channels and Amazon Prime. Ideal for families or groups.
